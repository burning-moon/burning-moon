Glossary

Cube of confusion: A place built of gravity to imprison dangerous beings (see: Mars). Cubes are scattered around galaxies and the official edges of the local universe.

The frozen Key: They say that it opens a Bulk Universe where -literally- everything is possible.

Head of Jagora: A head of a young woman will burn forever in hell to calm her sorrow.

Pendant of Lone Star: A pendand gives its owner protection in dangers.

The Bag: A small bag with a big grin face has finite options, it existed before the universe itself.
